GET INVOLVED!

From volunteering to hosting an event to proposing something we’ve never thought of, there are many ways you can bring your ingenuity and passion to Vancouver Design Week(end).
Please note that registration fees do apply to event hosting, but any registrations that are not approved will be refunded.

1 / Open Studios

Open the doors to your space, your process, and your unique design perspective. Open Studios offers a chance to bring fellow designers and the public into your world, and to show how your work shapes theirs.

Register to Open Your Studio

2 / Open Call

Local designers, design organizations, and design thinkers are invited to join the design conversation during the Vancouver Design Week(end). If you are positively contributing to the local design scene, and are interested in creating a design-related happening, please pitch your idea below.

Members of the VDW event administration will review your submission to ensure alignment with the VDW mission.

Propose an Open Call Event

3 / Tastings

Many food and beverage providers have made design a priority in their business, from branding to interiors to the offerings themselves. Tastings is a way to invite in a design-conscious audience to showcase what you've made. Tastings hosts are encouarged to sell tickets in advance to manage capacity and cover costs.

Tastings Inquiries: tastings@vancouverdesignwk.com

Register to Host a Tasting
