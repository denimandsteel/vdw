Vancouver Design Week is a week-long collection of events and programming that explore the unique design ecosystem of our city. Join the conversation. Learn and share. Show up and help shape Vancouver's design future.

Following the overwhelming response to VDW’s inaugural year in 2014, we’re taking this year to build a robust and sustainable foundation for future events.
