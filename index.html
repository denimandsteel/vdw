Vancouver
Design Week

May 7-13, 2018

We’re back with a full week of design programming for 2018, turning the city into a petri dish of design process, practice, and perspectives through independent and collaborative programming. From talks to tours to parties to pop-ups, design across disciplines will open its doors for conversations and celebrations of all scales. Uncover the unique design ecosystem that shapes, defines and sustains our city. Don’t miss the chance to join in, speak up and stand out in an important force that shapes our city and the world beyond.

Our Programming

1 / Design Studios

A unique opportunity to go behind the scenes with local design talent opening their doors to the public to share their spaces, perspectives and practices with the public and other creatives.

2 / Open Landmarks

A chance to explore the city’s best designed buildings and urban spaces through the eyes of the interdisciplinary teams that created them.

3 / Designed Tastings

a unique opportunity to hear what shaped and inspired some of Vancouver’s most distinctive tasting rooms (restaurants, cafes, bars, breweries) from the design teams that created them. Paired with signature amuse-bouches.

4 / Design Talks

A smattering of design discussions throughout the week to stimulate your creativity.

5 / Design Tours

guided by the most design- informed, curated tours will enhance your knowledge and experience of our city’s design talent.
