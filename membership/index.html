VDF Membership

Stand up for Vancouver’s design community with a VDF Membership

Great design is happening every day in Vancouver. It’s happening in solo passion projects and all-hands agency rollouts. It’s happening in subtle touches of beauty and bold new ways of thinking. The work of Vancouver’s design community is essential to how we all live and proper here. But it’s easy to lose sight of that bigger picture without a way of coming together.

The Vancouver Design Foundation is a volunteer-run not-for-profit dedicated to recognizing, celebrating, and advancing the [VISION/MISSION HERE]. As we look towards our third Vancouver Design Week festival and join a network of design week cities worldwide, you can play a part by becoming a member of the Vancouver Design Foundation.

Your membership helps make VDW happen, and raises your hand as someone who cares about the design work and career development. Over time, we’ll be rolling out a growing list of perks through the year, starting with:

  • Access to invite-only events and parties at VDW
  • Priority registration for core VDW events
  • Members-only job postings (for businesses and individuals)
  • A vote for Vancouver Design Foundation’s future board members
  • Logo recognition on our website (business only)

Please support this work to bring Vancouver designers and their work into the spotlight by becoming a member today.
